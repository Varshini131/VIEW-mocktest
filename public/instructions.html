General Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

1. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself.

2. The Questions Palette displayed on the right side of the screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:

  • You have not visited the question yet.
  • ✖ You have not answered the question.
  • ✔ You have answered the question.
  • ⌛ You have NOT answered the question, but have marked it for review.
  • ⌛✔ You have answered the question, but marked it for review.

Navigating to a Question:

3. To answer a question, do the following:

  • Click on the question number in the Question Palette to go to that numbered question directly.
  • Click on Save & Next to save your answer and go to the next question.
  • Click on Mark for Review & Next to mark for review and go to the next question.
