General Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

1. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner will show remaining time. When it reaches zero, the test will end automatically.

2. Question Palette symbols:

  • Not visited
  • ✖ Not answered
  • ✔ Answered
  • ⌛ Marked for review
  • ⌛✔ Answered & marked for review

Navigating Questions:

  • Click question number in palette to jump directly
  • Click Save & Next to save answer
  • Click Mark for Review & Next to review later
